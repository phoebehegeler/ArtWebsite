Dada Poem

  • Medium: Paper
    Art type: Dada Poem
    Date made:
For this assignment, we had to make a dada poem. Dada was an art movement in WW1 for artists to offend the political people, who they believed created a senseless war. It is abstract art, poetry and performance swirled together and it embraces the illogical and chaotic. It places the cutouts of words and puts them in a random order that makes no logical sense. Dada tried to undermine the main structures of rational, ordered society. Since the heart of reason is language, destroying words and disrupt language was the ultimate act of rebellion.

Steps

Step 1 : Firstly, I looked through the magazine to find interesting pictures and words in different fonts and sizes and colors. S

Step 2 : After cutting out the words I wanted, I arranged the words in a random order where the sentence didn't make any sense and I placed all the different sized and different colored words evenly spaced from each other. I also did not paste them in a straight line but instead made the line curved.

Step 3 : After cutting out the images, I arranged them in an interesting way. I cut out lips from a picture and added it on top of a different face and so the lips look abnormally big and funny. I also cut out an eye and gave a person only one eye in the center.
