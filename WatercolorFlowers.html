    Tiger Montage Self Portrait

Watercolor Flowers

  • Medium: Watercolor
    Art type:
For this assignment, had to make a sketchbook cover with our name on it.

Steps

Step 1 : Firstly, I brainstormed a bunch of ideas I had and then made 5 preliminary designs. The first one is a reflection of the moon and a bridge over the lake, the second one is a narrow gate with flowers over it, the third one is a girl in black and white standing in the gloomy breeze, the fourth one is a bicycle with flowers in the basket. I also experimented with different mediums such as pencil, color pencil and watercolors.



Step 2 : Since I felt like I tried relatively more complicated ideas in my previous preliminary designs that didn't turn out that well, I tried an easier one that I would turn out well. So for my 5th preliminary I simply used q-tips to paint a flower.

                                 

Step 3 : Since my art teacher said it was too simple and that I should add designs to the border, I added flower patterns to the borders. I also very lightly shaded the background with pink and yellow color pencils.


Reflection

Something that turned out extremely well in this artwork was the borders with the indian flower patterns. Something I can improve on next time is choose leaves that would suit the flower since the ones I drew most certainly did not. I faced a lot of failure which making my prelimnary designs. Although I put in a lot of effort and time, the designs did not turn out as well as I had expected. To be fair, I had chosen more complicated designs but I was still disappointed. I dealt with the failure by trying a preliminary design which was rather simple to do - the opposite the other ones I had tried. This one turned out really well. I made the decision to choose the simpler one and spend more time perfecting it and adding details to it than choose a harder one which would probably not turn out well. This was a great idea because I also realised that when you look at art, you don't see its difficulty level and how well a person did based on that but rather you look at its beauty. I learnt that even if an artwork was simple to make, it can outshine and be more beautiful than some of the more complicated artworks.
