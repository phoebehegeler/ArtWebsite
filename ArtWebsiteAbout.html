ABOUT



Hello! Thank you so much for taking the time to look at my website. I am a 16 year old student in IB who loves art and I created this website to show my passion but also to show the experience of taking HS art as an elective in IB. I coded this entire website using HTML, CSS and Javascript which was challenging but fun. I would like to thank everyone who helped me create this website. I would like to thank my personal project supervisor and Software Design teacher who helped me when I was struggling with the code and helped me enjoy coding. I would also like to thank my art teacher who helped me enjoy my art journey, always encouraged me and loved all of my artworks - I know you were excited to see my website so I hope you like it! I would like to thank my family who encouraged my love for art and believed in me even when I didn't believe that I could do well in some of my artworks or in coding. Lastly I would like to thank all of readers, thank you for visiting my website and enjoying my art!
